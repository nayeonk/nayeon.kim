I currently work full-time as a Senior Lecturer at the Viterbi School of Engineering at USC. I teach web development to students of all majors. It was not an easy path, but I'm so glad I persevered through it.

Even though I now teach full-time, I still consult and develop projects on the side to keep my feet wet in the latest web technologies.
